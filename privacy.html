Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application Tech Media Publisher ("the App") handles information when used with the TikTok API.

1. Scope

The App is a personal automation tool operated by the account owner of the TikTok channel @techmedia_6. It is not offered as a service to other users.

2. Data the App Accesses

Via the TikTok API, the App may access:

  • Basic profile information (open_id, display name, avatar) of the authorizing TikTok account
  • Public video list and statistics of the authorizing account
  • Permission to upload and publish videos to the authorizing account

The App only accesses data of TikTok accounts that have explicitly authorized it.

3. How Data Is Used

Accessed data is used solely to enable the App's core functions: scheduling, uploading, and analyzing videos on the authorizing account. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

4. Storage

OAuth tokens and related metadata are stored locally on the operator's device. No backend server receives or retains user data.

5. Retention

Data is retained only as long as the App is in use. Tokens can be revoked at any time by the authorizing user via TikTok's account settings.
